# Research Topic

LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Research Goal

To identify recurring frameworks, content patterns, audience-building tactics, and demand generation strategies used by leading B2B SaaS operators on LinkedIn.

## Expert Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on:

- Active LinkedIn publishing
- Practical B2B SaaS experience
- Evidence of audience growth through organic content
- Strong reputation among B2B marketers and founders
- Clear, repeatable content frameworks
